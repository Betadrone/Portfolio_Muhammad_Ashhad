Autobiography

Each person possesses a unique identity, coming in diverse forms and sizes, and guided by distinct philosophies. One such individual is Muhammad Ashhad, who, from his earliest days, was captivated by the enchanting world of games and the narratives they spun. He found joy in diving headfirst into these virtual realms, whether through active participation or as a spectator of others' adventures. This fixation would evolve to encompass stories in all their forms, yet games remained the most intimate medium for experiencing narratives.

Motivated by this passion, Ashhad's inner fire pushed him to venture into game development. Armed with basic knowledge of C++ programming, he created his first gameâ€”a text-based boss RPG battle. As he deepened his grasp of object-oriented programming, he completed two more projects: a Frogger replica in C++ and an infinite platformer in JavaScript.

Surprisingly, Ashhad's journey wasn't always destined for game development. His life's trajectory took a different course until a pivotal moment when his father urged him to explore a game development course at the local college. He believed in equipping oneself with the necessary knowledge before embarking on any endeavor. This opportunity solidified Ashhad's resolve, prompting him to seize this chance.

During his training, Ashhad gained exposure to collaborative work. He assumed various roles, from project manager to presentation designer and even the primary presenter. The challenges of group dynamics, including ensuring everyone contributed equitably and resolving unexpected obstacles, tested his resolve. However, he faced each challenge with determination, ultimately surmounting them.

Recognizing the importance of self-reflection, Ashhad actively sought to identify and hone his strengths while addressing his weaknesses. He identified consistency, time management, problem deconstruction, and abstraction as his foundational skills, all of which were bolstered by his love for puzzles and mysteries. His problem-solving prowess allowed him to break down complex coding challenges into manageable segments, leading to more effective solutions.

Viewing coding as a grand puzzle, Ashhad found immense satisfaction in programming. His proficiency spanned a range of languages, from C++, C, C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to Python and Java. He also gained experience with Phaser and Unity, equipping him with a diverse set of tools.

One question may linger in your mind: what is his ultimate goal? For every query, there is an answer. Ashhad's ambition is to create games and, ultimately, establish his own company that crafts profoundly captivating stories that resonate with people, irrespective of the medium. To achieve this dream, he seeks your support and collaboration.
