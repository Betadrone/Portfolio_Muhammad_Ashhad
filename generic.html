About Me

Have you ever met an anxiety-driven optimist with a profound love for stories? Well, now you have. I'm Muhammad Ashhad, born in Ottawa and raised in Cornwall, where I was immersed in the world of coding from a young age, thanks to my father's influence. My unique blend of interests emerged early on—I adored math and physics, and I was captivated by the art of storytelling and gaming.

Growing up in the company of an engineering-minded father, I developed a distinct approach to problem-solving, rooted in computational thinking. Combined with my penchant for puzzle-solving, this skill set has enabled me to tackle coding challenges in projects like recreating a C++ Frogger replica and developing a JavaScript infinite platforming game.

My ultimate goal is to create games and, eventually, my own company that delivers stories so captivating and profound that they leave an indelible mark on everyone, regardless of the medium. But to achieve this dream, I need your help and collaboration.

I'm curious—have you ever encountered a piece of media or a game that has left a lasting impression on you? If so, I'd love to hear about it. Let's connect and share our passion for storytelling and game development.
